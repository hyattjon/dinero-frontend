How It Works

Credit Card Matcher helps you find the best credit card for your unique spending habits. When you click "Connect with Plaid," you securely link your bank account using Plaid’s trusted platform. Our app then analyzes your past year of transactions (read-only, never stored) and compares your real spending patterns with hundreds of credit card offers from rewardscc.com. We calculate how much cashback or rewards you could have earned with each card, so you can see which cards would have given you the most value—no guesswork, just data-driven recommendations.

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We never store your bank credentials or transaction data.
